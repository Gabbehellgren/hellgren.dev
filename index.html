About me

Hi, my name is Oscar! I own the domain hellgren.dev and I’m both the designer and developer behind this site. I was born in 2007 in Sweden, and right now I’m in my senior year of high school. After graduation, I plan to continue studying at university with the goal of becoming an engineer.

I’ve always been into tech — in my free time I enjoy coding and building different projects, and I’m also really into 3D printing. Outside of tech, I spend a lot of time training and competing in canoeing. I usually work out once a day and have competed in the Nordic Championships a few times. I started paddling back in 2018, and my dream is to one day reach the Olympics. Starting from the autumn regatta in 2025, I’ll be competing as a senior.

I wouldn’t say I’m fluent in any programming language yet, but I feel pretty confident in a few, including:

  • Python
  • C#
  • HTML & CSS

I’ve also been learning my way around Linux Ubuntu without a graphical interface, which has been a fun challenge and a great learning experience.
